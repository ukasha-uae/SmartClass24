## WAEC e-Learning URL Patterns

Based on the site structure, here are URL patterns to access Physics questions:

### Paper 1 (Objective/MCQ) - 2023
- Base URL: `https://www.waeconline.org.ng/e-learning/Physics/`
- Pattern: `Phys230mq[1-50].html` (for 2023, Q1-Q50)

### Paper 2 (Essay) - 2023  
- Pattern: `Phys230eq[1-12].html` (for 2023, Q1-Q12)

### Other Years

## Verification Process

1. **Access WAEC e-Learning**: https://www.waeconline.org.ng/e-learning/Physics/
2. **Navigate by year** (2023, 2022, etc.)
3. **Match our questions** to WAEC questions
4. **Extract question numbers** from URLs or page content
5. **Update physics.ts** with verified information
