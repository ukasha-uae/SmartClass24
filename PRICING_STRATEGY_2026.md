5. **Demo Videos**: "Launch Your Branded Platform in 24 Hours" viral content

**CAC Target**: $1,000-$2,000 per school (10-15% of annual contract value)

### **Sales Funnel**

**Stage 1: Awareness**
- Homepage "For Schools" section
- LinkedIn/Google ads
- Case studies (Wisdom Warehouse)

**Stage 2: Interest**
- Free 30-day trial (no credit card)
- Live demo with school branding
- Custom curriculum preview

**Stage 3: Decision**
- Demo call with founder/sales team
- Custom proposal with ROI calculations
- Reference check with existing clients

**Stage 4: Purchase**
- Simple contract (annual preferred, monthly available)
- 50% setup fee upfront, 50% at launch
- Onboarding starts within 48 hours

**Stage 5: Retention**
- Dedicated account manager (Growth+ plans)
- Quarterly check-ins
- Annual contract renewal with discount (15% off)

### **Churn Mitigation**
- **30-day notice** required for cancellation
- **Data export** provided within 7 days
- **Annual contracts** with 15% discount (lock-in)
- **Success metrics** tracking (login rates, quiz completion, NPS)
- **Proactive support** for at-risk accounts (usage < 50% of expected)

## üìä Key Metrics to Track

### **Revenue Metrics**
- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- **ARPU** (Average Revenue Per User - per school)
- **Setup Fee Revenue** (one-time)
- **Add-On Revenue** (% of total)
- **Churn Rate** (monthly & annual)

### **Sales Metrics**
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value per school)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio** (target: 3:1 or higher)
- **Sales Cycle Length** (days from demo to close)
- **Win Rate** (% of demos that convert)
- **Pipeline Value** (total value of deals in progress)

### **Operational Metrics**
- **Onboarding Time** (days from contract to launch)
- **Time to Value** (days until first student login)
- **Support Ticket Volume** (per school)
- **Uptime %** (target: 99.5%+)
- **NPS** (Net Promoter Score from schools)
