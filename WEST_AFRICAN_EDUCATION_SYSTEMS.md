### ğŸ‡³ğŸ‡¬ **NIGERIA**
**Structure:**
- **Primary School**: Primary 1-6 (Ages 6-11)
- **JSS**: Junior Secondary School (JSS 1-3, Ages 12-14)
- **SSS**: Senior Secondary School (SSS 1-3, Ages 15-17)

**Exams:**
- JSCE - Junior Secondary Certificate Examination (after JSS)
- SSCE/WASSCE - Senior School Certificate Examination (after SSS)
- NECO - National Examinations Council (alternative to WASSCE)
- JAMB - Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (for university entrance)

**Conducting Bodies:** WAEC, NECO

**Key Differences:**
- Nigeria uses JSS/SSS instead of JHS/SHS
- Nigeria has NECO as alternative to WASSCE
- Nigeria has JAMB for tertiary admissions

### ğŸ‡¸ğŸ‡± **SIERRA LEONE**
**Structure:**
- **Primary School**: Class 1-6 (Ages 6-11)
- **JSS**: Junior Secondary School (JSS 1-3, Ages 12-14)
- **SSS**: Senior Secondary School (SSS 1-3, Ages 15-17)

**Exams:**
- NPSE - National Primary School Examination (after Primary)
- BECE - Basic Education Certificate Examination (after JSS)
- WASSCE - West African Senior School Certificate Examination (after SSS)

**Conducting Body:** WAEC

**Key Features:**
- Same JSS/SSS as Nigeria
- Uses BECE like Ghana (but at JSS level)
- Has primary school exit exam (NPSE)

### ğŸ‡±ğŸ‡· **LIBERIA**
**Structure:**
- **Elementary School**: Grades 1-6 (Ages 6-11)
- **Junior High School**: Grades 7-9 (Ages 12-14)
- **Senior High School**: Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-17)

**Exams:**
- NHSSE - National High School Service Examination (Grade 9)
- WASSCE - West African Senior School Certificate Examination (Grade 12)

**Conducting Body:** WAEC

**Key Differences:**
- Uses "Grades" instead of "Class" or "Primary/JSS/SSS"
- Calls it "Junior High" and "Senior High" (like Ghana)
- NHSSE instead of BECE/JSCE

- **Upper Basic**: Grades 7-9 (Ages 12-14)
- **Senior Secondary**: Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-17)

**Exams:**
- GABECE - Gambia Basic Education Certificate Examination (after Upper Basic)
- WASSCE - West African Senior School Certificate Examination (after Senior Secondary)

**Conducting Body:** WAEC

**Key Differences:**
- Unique "Lower Basic" and "Upper Basic" terminology
- GABECE instead of BECE/JSCE
- "Senior Secondary" (not SSS or SHS)
